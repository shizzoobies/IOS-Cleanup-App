Frequently asked questions

Are my photos uploaded to the internet?

Originals never leave your device. For AI analysis, SwipeClean sends a small compressed thumbnail (max 512px, JPEG 70%) to our backend. The thumbnail is processed immediately and discarded -- nothing is stored on our servers. Faces are blurred and GPS data is stripped before upload, by default.

I accidentally deleted a photo. Can I get it back?

Yes. SwipeClean never permanently deletes photos. Deleted photos move to your iPhone's Recently Deleted album, where they stay for 30 days. Open the Photos app, go to Albums > Recently Deleted, and recover anything you want.

How do I restore my Pro subscription on a new device?

Open SwipeClean, tap the Upgrade button, then tap "Restore purchases" at the bottom of the paywall. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iOS Settings > Apple ID (your name at the top) > Subscriptions > SwipeClean Pro. Tap Cancel Subscription. You keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

What is the Big Cleanup Pack?

The Big Cleanup Pack is a one-time purchase that gives you unlimited swipes for 7 days -- no subscription, no auto-renewal. Great if you just want to do one big cleanup without committing to a subscription.

The app says "Could not load library." What do I do?

SwipeClean needs access to your photo library. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > SwipeClean and choose "Full Access." Then reopen the app.

I hit my 50 free swipes. When do they reset?

Free swipes reset every day at midnight. If you need more right away, you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited swipes, or grab the Big Cleanup Pack for a 7-day unlimited pass.

How does duplicate detection work?

SwipeClean uses on-device perceptual hashing to find visually similar photos. This works entirely on your device -- no photos are uploaded for duplicate detection. It catches near-duplicates like burst shots and slight crops, not just exact copies.
